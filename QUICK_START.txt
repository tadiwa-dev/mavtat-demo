📱 MAVTAT Motors - Quick Start Guide

🔗 Open the app: https://mavtat-demo.vercel.app

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✨ MAIN FEATURES:

🏠 DASHBOARD
• See total fleet, active vehicles, maintenance count
• View recent activity & revenue summary

📋 REGISTRY
• View all your vehicles
• 🔍 Search by name or license plate
• Click "Status" to change vehicle status (Available/Active/Rented/Maintenance/Low Fuel/Inactive)
• Click "Update km" to update mileage
• Click "+ New" to add a new vehicle

📊 REPORTS
• Financial breakdown per vehicle
• Export to Excel (CSV) or PDF
• See profit/revenue for each vehicle

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🎮 QUICK ACTIONS:

➕ ADD VEHICLE
1. Click "+ New" on Dashboard or Registry
2. Enter: Make/Model, License Plate, Type, Mileage
3. Click "Save Vehicle"

📍 UPDATE STATUS
1. Go to Registry
2. Click "Status" button
3. Choose new status
4. Click "Update Status"

⛽ UPDATE MILEAGE
1. Go to Registry
2. Click "Update km" button
3. Enter new mileage (must be higher)
4. Click "Update"

📥 EXPORT REPORTS
1. Go to Reports
2. Click "Excel (CSV)" or "PDF Report"
3. Download & share with team

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💡 TIPS:
✓ Works on desktop, tablet & mobile
✓ All changes are instant
✓ Data resets when you refresh (demo mode)
✓ Use search to find vehicles quickly
